News

This application allows you to view COVID-19 data of select counties in the United States. You will be allowed to view the data of any county, but are not allowed to manipulate any data.

If you wish to view COVID-19 data of a county, start by filling out the relevant information above the map. Then check the map, you should be able to view data pertaining to your chosen county.
More infomation can be found on the About Us page.

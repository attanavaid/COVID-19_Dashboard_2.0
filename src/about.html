Who Are We?

We are a team of students that wanted to make COVID-19 data easily accessible by county population.
We created this application, because we were determined to improve the way data is shown to us. Our determination started at the beginning of September 2021. Since then, we have been working hard to create something innovating and effective in a short span of time. We envisioned a future where there are no obstacles that prevent people from looking up COVID-19 for counties.

Community

This application was designed by UMBC students. So the first few users of this application are all from UMBC. And they mainly viewed COVID-19 data of Baltimore County. This county was also used as the basis for development.
From all the support and encouragement we received at UMBC, we decided to reach further and bring the support and care we received from the community of UMBC, to more people. As our community expands, more and more patients get the care and management they really deserve and need to improve their health.
